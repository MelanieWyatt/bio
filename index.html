2017 Prom Picture
Saying Goodbye For College

My older sister Allison is the most positive influence in my life. I am choosing her because despite everything she has been through, she has still managed to be successful and kept a smile on her face. She has taught me that no matter what life throws at you to knock you down you can get right back up and be even stronger.

Picture Perfect Selfie

Growing up with her we fought like cats and dogs. I might’ve accidentally broken her arm in a fight one day and told her she could hit me back. She was in a cast for a few months and when she got out of it – she hit me with it. She taught me how to defend myself.

Before a varsity XC race

Ally has taught me how to make friends for life. She was the first friend I ever had and will be my best friend until the day I die. Originally, she made me go introduce myself to her friends so I would not be lonely. She reminds me every day how to be kind to everybody no matter what.

Sunday Funday

She has been through so much in her 19 years of living that it really inspires me to not take anything I have for granted. When she was diagnosed with a functional movement disorder when she was younger (which makes her have tremors when she’s sick or stressed), I saw she started working harder than ever to make sure she was just like the other kids and I was so inspired. She started running cross country when she was a freshman to help her get over her disorder and it made her so strong.

After she was put in a wheelchair for not being able to walk (due to her bad tremors in her legs), the doctor said it’d take her over a year to get back being able to walk normal again. She was so determined to prove them wrong that within 6 months she was walking again. That made me so inspired. She is the definition of a girl who is a fighter.

Whenever I am sick, she is the first one to try to make me feel better and make sure I will be okay and makes sure I am not fixing to die. I can truly count on her for every single thing. If I have a problem – I know I can go to her and she will have a solution. If I want to fight someone – she will be there and will be able to calm me down. She is the best big sister I truly could’ve ever asked for. I am so blessed to have her as my big sister and that I will join her in college at University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.
